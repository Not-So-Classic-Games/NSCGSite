About Us

We design and build games that value creativity over convention. Our team is committed to staying independent, not just in name, but in philosophy. That means no exploitative monetization, no soulless or cash-grab design, and no reliance on generative AI. Every line of code, every piece of art, and every mechanic is made by real people with real intent.

Studio Values

No crunch cultureWe believe great games don't require burning out the people who make them.
Player-First mindsetDesign decisions are made with respect for the player's time, intelligence, and enjoyment.
Handmade, alwaysEvery asset is created by real artists, no generative AI, no shortcuts.
No pay-to-winWe reject manipulative monetization. If it feels scummy, we don't do it.
Talent and creativity firstOur team’s originality drives every project. We might follow trends, but creativity always leads.
