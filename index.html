About Us

We design and build games that value creativity over convention. Our company is committed to staying independent, not just in name, but in philosophy. That means no exploitative monetization, no soulless or cash-grab design, and no reliance on generative AI. Every line of code, every piece of art, and every mechanic is made by real people with real intent.
